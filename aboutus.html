Our Story

Founded in 2018, Luxe Nails & Spa began as a dream to create a sanctuary where clients could experience truly luxurious nail care. Our founder, Sarah Chen, envisioned a space where artistry meets relaxation, and every visit feels like a special occasion.

What started as a small boutique salon has grown into a beloved destination for those who appreciate attention to detail, premium products, and exceptional service. We believe that beautiful nails should be accessible to everyone, without compromising on quality or experience.
